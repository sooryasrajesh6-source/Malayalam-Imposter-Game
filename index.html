Find The Imposter

Malayalam Party Game

How to Play

Add minimum 3 players.

Most players get the same Malayalam word.

One player gets a different word. That player is the imposter.

Discuss, ask questions, vote, then click Finish Game.

Game will not finish automatically.

Add Players

Ask questions and find who got the different word.

Vote Imposter

Tap player names to vote. Game finishes only after clicking Finish Game.
